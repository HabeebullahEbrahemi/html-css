Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a landlocked country located in South-Central Asia. It shares borders with Pakistan, Iran, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, and China. The country has a rich cultural history and has been a center of trade and conquest for centuries.

Kabul

Kabul is the capital and largest city of Afghanistan with a population of nearly 6 million people.
It is located in the eastern part of the country and serves as the political, cultural, and economic center.
The city has many districts, each with its own local markets and landmarks.
Kabul is known for its historical gardens, vibrant bazaars, and scenic mountain surroundings.

Bagh Bala

Bagh Bala is a historic palace and garden located on a hilltop in Kabul City. It offers a beautiful panoramic view of the city and is a popular spot for picnics and tourism. It dates back to the 19th century during the time of Amir Abdur Rahman Khan.

India

India is a vast South Asian country known for its diverse culture, languages, religions, and historical landmarks. It is the world's largest democracy and the seventh-largest country by land area.

Delhi

Delhi is the capital of India and one of the oldest existing cities in the world.
It has a population of over 18 million people and is divided into New Delhi (the capital city) and Old Delhi.
Delhi is famous for its rich history, heritage buildings, bustling streets, and diverse cuisine.

India Gate

India Gate is a 42-meter high war memorial located in the heart of New Delhi.
It was built in memory of the 70,000 Indian soldiers who died in World War I.
The structure is an iconic symbol of Delhi and a popular spot for both locals and tourists, especially in the evenings when it's beautifully lit.
